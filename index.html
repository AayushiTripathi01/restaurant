At RS, we’re serving up more than 2 years. In fact, RS Famous for its italian food is one of our unexpected specialties. Our signature recipe, that we have perfected and is rubbed with a secret blend of seasonings, cured and roasted on a rotisserie with pecan wood for up to six hours before it’s topped with RS signature herb-garlic butter Pasta, then carved tableside.
